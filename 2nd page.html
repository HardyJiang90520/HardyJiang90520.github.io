The RC Airplanes

One thing that I like is the RC airplanes. In RC airplanes, we use transmitters to control the airplanes. The transmitter we usually use is a type of special transmitter that send something like pwm signals in 2.4G. Also, we use a special type of receivers as well. The RC airplanes are using servos to control the direction of there the RC airplanes fly and use brushless motors to control the speed of the RC airplanes. The reason why I like RC airplanes is because you can enjoy the freedom like a bird in the sky. Seriously, I think I`m still an beginner of the RC airplanes.  :/
